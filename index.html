About Our Academy

Panamerican Taekwondo Academy is dedicated to providing world-class martial arts training in a safe, supportive, and disciplined environment. Our experienced instructors are committed to helping students of all ages and skill levels achieve their goals.

We offer comprehensive training in multiple disciplines including Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Taekwondo, Muay Thai, and MMA. Our programs focus not only on physical fitness and self-defense skills but also on character development, discipline, and respect.

Our Programs

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Learn the art of ground fighting and submission grappling. Perfect for self-defense and competition.

  • Beginner to Advanced Classes
  • Competition Training
  • Self-Defense Techniques

Taekwondo

Master dynamic kicks, strikes, and forms while developing discipline and mental focus.

  • Traditional Forms & Techniques
  • Olympic-Style Sparring
  • Belt Progression System

Muay Thai

Experience the "Art of Eight Limbs" with striking techniques using fists, elbows, knees, and shins.

  • Traditional Thai Boxing
  • Pad Work & Bag Training
  • Clinch Techniques

MMA

Comprehensive mixed martial arts training combining striking, grappling, and ground fighting.

  • Stand-Up & Ground Game
  • Competition Preparation
  • Strength & Conditioning
