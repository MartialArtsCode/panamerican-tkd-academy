About Panamerican Taekwondo Academy

Welcome to the Panamerican Taekwondo Academy, where tradition meets excellence. Our academy has been dedicated to teaching authentic Taekwondo for over 20 years, fostering discipline, respect, and physical fitness in students of all ages.

We follow the World Taekwondo Federation (WT) and Global Traditional Taekwondo Federation (GTTF) curriculums and emphasize both the physical and mental aspects of martial arts training. Our programs are designed to build character, confidence, and self-defense skills.

Training Programs

Power Up

All Ages

Created for neurodivergent students including those with ASD, ADHD, anxiety or sensory processing differences. This program helps each child develop confidence, coordination, focus, and emotional regulation through Taekwondo.

  • Basic kicks and punches
  • Balance and coordination
  • Listening skills
  • Social interaction

Little Ninjas

Ages 5-9

Comprehensive Taekwondo training focusing on technique, discipline, and character development.

  • Traditional forms (Poomsae)
  • Sparring techniques
  • Self-defense
  • Basic Competition

Dragons

Ages 10-17

Youth-focused training emphasizing leadership, discipline, and practical self-defense skills.

  • Advanced competition
  • Demonstration techniques
  • Self-defense applications
  • Mental discipline

Core

Ages 17+

Adult-focused training emphasizing fitness, stress relief, and practical self-defense skills.

  • Cardiovascular fitness
  • Flexibility training
  • Self-defense applications
  • Traditional Forms (Poomsae)
