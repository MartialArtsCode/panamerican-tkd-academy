Our Programs

Power Up

Ages 13+

Advanced training for teens and adults. Build strength, discipline, and self-defense skills through traditional Taekwondo techniques combined with modern fitness approaches.

  • Self-Defense Mastery
  • Physical Fitness
  • Mental Discipline
  • Competition Training

Little Ninjas

Ages 4-6

Our youngest warriors learn the basics of martial arts through fun, engaging activities that develop coordination, focus, and confidence in a safe environment.

  • Motor Skills Development
  • Following Instructions
  • Social Skills
  • Basic Techniques

Dragons

Ages 7-9

Building on fundamentals, Dragons develop advanced techniques, leadership skills, and prepare for competitive Taekwondo while fostering respect and perseverance.

  • Advanced Techniques
  • Leadership Training
  • Character Development
  • Tournament Preparation

Core

Ages 10-12

The bridge to advanced training, Core students master traditional forms, sparring techniques, and begin their journey toward black belt excellence.

  • Form Mastery
  • Sparring Skills
  • Self-Confidence
  • Black Belt Preparation
